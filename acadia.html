About

Acadia National Park undergoes stunning fall colors each October. The park has breathtaking views, particularly during the fall foliage season. From the iconic Cadaliac Mountain, providing panaramic views of its landscape, to its beautiful reflecting lakes. The park offers immersive expierences amid the vibrant foliage, making Acadia National Park a must-visit destination.
